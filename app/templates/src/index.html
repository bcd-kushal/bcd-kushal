About Me

Hey there, I'm Kushal Kumar

Hey there! I'm a designer, developer, artist, reader and blogger. I made this site to showcase all of what I can do and plan to do. I got into design because I consider myself a pseudo-perfectionist, if that's even a word? As in, I hate to see things 'not look good'. So I set out on a journey to make products that people use that 'look great', and I'm forever on that path. I got into development because tech-stuffs have always fascinated me. Seeing code getting converted to things we use regularly, was infatuating and that's where my quest to invent began. I'm an avid reader as vocabulary is a strong key-aspect to communicate with others and share my passion to the world. I created this website so I could showcase all this and through this process, make it easier for you to connect with me. If you like what you see, head over to the contact section and send me a text. I would love to hear from you!
